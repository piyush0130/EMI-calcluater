What is an EMI?

Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) is a fixed payment amount made by a borrower to a lender at a specified date each calendar month. EMIs are used to pay off both interest and principal each month so that over a specified number of years, the loan is paid off in full.

How does the EMI Calculator work?

This free calculator helps you find the EMI for home loans, car loans, or personal loans. It uses the mathematical formula: E = P x R x (1+R)^N / [(1+R)^N-1]. Simply enter your total loan amount or product price, the downpayment you are making, your annual bank interest rate, and the duration of the loan in months to get accurate results instantly.
