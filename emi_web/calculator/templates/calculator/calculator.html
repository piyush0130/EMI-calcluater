What is an EMI?

An Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) is a fixed payment amount made by a borrower to a lender at a specified date each calendar month. EMIs are used to pay off both interest and principal each month so that over a specified number of years, the loan is paid off in full. By using an online EMI calculator, borrowers can easily plan their monthly budgets.

How does the Loan EMI Calculator work?

Our fast and free EMI calculator helps you find the monthly repayment amount for home loans, car loans, or personal loans. For reducing balance calculation, it uses the mathematical formula: E = P x R x (1+R)^N / [(1+R)^N-1]. We also offer a Flat Rate calculation method commonly used in some auto loans and personal loans. Simply enter your total loan amount or product price, the downpayment you are making, your annual bank interest rate, and the duration of the loan in months to get accurate results instantly.
